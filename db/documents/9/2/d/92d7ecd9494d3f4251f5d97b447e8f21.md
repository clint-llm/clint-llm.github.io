Muscle biopsy is required in certain cases where genetic testing comes out to be inconclusive. Microscopy can show increased internalized nuclei (boxcar appearance), ring fibers, sarcoplasmic masses, and type 1 fiber atrophy with a pyknotic clump and type 2 fiber hypertrophy in DM1 and type 2 fiber atrophy in DM2.

**Electron Microscopy Appearance:**Sarcoplasmic masses composed of disorganized myofibrils, dilated sarcoplasmic reticulum, and free ribosomes.
