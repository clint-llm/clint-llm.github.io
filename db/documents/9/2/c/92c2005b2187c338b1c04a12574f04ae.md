After discussing the complications associated with bariatric surgery, the decision to proceed with surgical intervention may seem intimidating to patients. It is important to note that obesity is an independent risk factor for health conditions including stroke (CVA), coronary artery disease (CAD), OSA, hyperlipidemia (HLD), diabetes mellitus (DM), hypertension (HTN), degenerative joint disease, asthma, cancer, and depression.

Improvement in comorbidities is noticed as early as six months following weight loss surgery/ Patients have improved longevity with an 80% drop in annual mortality and a 50% reduction in overall mortality.
