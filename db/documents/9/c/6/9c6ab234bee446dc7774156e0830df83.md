**Adenocarcinoma of Lung**

Lung cancer is the most common cause of cancer and the leading cause of death in men in the world and the second leading cause in women.

**Adenocarcinoma of Breast**

Breast Cancer is the second most common cause of cancer worldwide and the most common cancer in women.

**Adenocarcinoma of Prostate**

Adenocarcinoma of the Prostate is the second most common cancer in men worldwide. It is the third leading cause of death in the United States.

**Adenocarcinoma of Colon/Rectum**

Colorectal cancers are the third most common cancer in males and second in females.
