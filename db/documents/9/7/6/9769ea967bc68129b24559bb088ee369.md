The differential diagnosis of primary tumors of omentum is

- Fibrosarcoma

- Spindle cell sarcoma

- Liposarcoma

- Leiomyoma

- Hemangiopericytoma

- Lipoma

- Desmoid tumor

- Fibroma

- Mesothelioma

- Myosarcoma

- Rhabdomyosarcoma

- Leiomyoblastoma

- Endothelioma.

These tumors can be mistaken for pelvic tumors, ovarian tumors, and GISTs.
