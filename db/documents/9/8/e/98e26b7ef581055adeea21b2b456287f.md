Tetramine has the chemical formula C4H8NO4S2, and its chemical name is tetramethylenedisulfoteramine. Other names include TMDT, TETS, Dushuqiang (very strong rat poison), 4-2-4, and Sanbudado (takesÂ 3 steps and keels over).

Tetramine is odorless, colorless, and persistent in the body and the environment.

Thus, symptom onset is rapid, with most cases showing toxicity within 30 minutes of ingestion.
