**Preoperative**

- Skin marker

- 1% lidocaine with 1:100,000 epinephrine in 3 cc syringes with 30 gauge, 0.5 inch needles

- Isopropanol, chlorhexidine gluconate, or povidone iodine for skin cleansing

**Intraoperatively**

- #15 blade, #15C blade, or #6700 beaver blade

- Adson-Brown and 0.5 mm Castroviejo forceps

- Kaye blepharoplasty scissors

- Suture scissors

- Bipolar electrocautery

- Guthrie skin hooks

- Castroviejo needle driver

- Absorbable (such as poligelcaprone or polyglactin) and non-absorbable (such as nylon or polypropylene) suture

**Postoperatively**

- Bacitracin ointment, or:

- Skin glue, or:

- Adhesive and sterile tape strips
