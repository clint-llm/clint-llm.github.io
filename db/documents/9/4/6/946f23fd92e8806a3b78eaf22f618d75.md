The common differentials for CTCL are erythroderma like atopic/contact dermatitis, drug eruptions, erythrodermic psoriasis, lichen planus, cutaneous B-cell lymphoma, subcutaneous panniculitis t-cell lymphoma.

PTCL differentials include other subtypes of T-cell lymphoma, B-cell lymphoma, granulomatous histiocytosis, paracortical hyperplasia.
