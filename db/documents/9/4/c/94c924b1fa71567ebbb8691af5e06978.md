Vocal cord paralysis can lead to the following complications:

- Altered voice quality

- Weak cough

- Difficulty swallowing

- Feeding difficulty

- Hoarseness

- Stridor

- Dyspnea

- Aspiration risk

- Airway obstruction

- Granuloma Formation

- Arytenoid Chondritis

- Reduced quality of life associated with tracheostomy

- Laser related airway fire

- Scar formation
