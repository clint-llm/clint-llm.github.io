Opioids are medications used to manage and treat pain, cough, or diarrhea. This activity outlines the indications, actions, and contraindications for opioids. This activity will also highlight the mechanism of action, adverse event profile, and other key factors pertinent to the interprofessional healthcare team members in managing patients using an opioid and in light of the opioid crisis.

**Objectives:**
- Identify the mechanism of action of opioid drugs.
- Describe the adverse effects of opioids.
- Outline and explain the appropriate monitoring necessary when using opioid therapy.
- Summarize interprofessional team strategies for improving care coordination and communication to regulate opioid use and improve outcomes.
