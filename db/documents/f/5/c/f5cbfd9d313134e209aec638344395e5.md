**Traumatic Retroperitoneal Hematoma**

- Acute abdomen

- Perforated viscus

- Solid organ injury

- Vascular injury

- Pelvic fracture

- Abdominal compartment syndrome

**Non-traumatic Retroperitoneal Hematoma**

- GI bleeding

- Musculoskeletal pain

- Appendicitis

- Cholecystitis

- Diverticulitis

- Abdominal aortic aneurysm

- Pancreatitis

- Pyelonephritis
