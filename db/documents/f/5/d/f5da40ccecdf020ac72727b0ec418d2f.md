Choledocholithiasis is the presence of gallstones in the common bile duct. It presents with right upper abdominal pain. Typically, the pain has a peculiar pattern and timing for an individual patient. Eating a fatty meal is a common trigger for gallbladder contraction, and most patients report pain after eating a fatty meal. However, in a significant proportion of patients, the pain can be nocturnal.
