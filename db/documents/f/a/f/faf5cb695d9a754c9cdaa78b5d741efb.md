**Angioimmunoblastic T-cell Lymphoma**

They are classified according to Lugano classification for non-Hodgkin lymphoma. Divided into four stages: Stage I is a single lymph node region or one extra lymphatic region or site. Stage II is two lymph node regions on the same side of the diaphragm or with localized involvement of an extra lymphatic organ. Stage III refers to lymph node involvement on both sides of the diaphragm. Stage 4 refers to the widespread involvement of one or more extra lymphatic organs (lung, bone marrow).
