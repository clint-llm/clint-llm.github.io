The prevalence of CF differs by geographical background and ethnicity. It is commonly seen in Whites with an incidence of approximately 1 in 2000 to 3000 live births compared to an incidence of 1 in 4000 to10 000 and 1 in 15000 to 20000 live births in Latin American and African American populations, respectively. The prevalence of the disease in other racial groups is not well studied.
