Clinical variants of actinic keratoses include: hyperkeratotic AK, atrophic AK, actinic cheilitis, pigmented AK, lichenoid AK, and cutaneous horn.

The diagnosis of AK is primarily a clinical diagnosis. Other skin lesions considered in the differential include solar lentigo, seborrheic keratosis, verruca vulgaris, verruca plana, discoid lupus erythematosus, and squamous cell carcinoma.
