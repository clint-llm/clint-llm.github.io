Several anatomical and physiological concepts are essential to fully comprehend the various techniques of myocardial protection. This activity explains the fundamental concepts and the use of myocardial protection procedures and techniques by the interprofessional team.

**Objectives:**
- Explain the original foundation concepts behind myocardial protection.
- Describe the difference between cardioplegic and non-cardioplegic techniques of myocardial protection.
- Outline the various techniques available to deliver cardioplegic myocardial protection.
- Review some interprofessional team strategies that can improve outcomes in patients who require myocardial protection.
