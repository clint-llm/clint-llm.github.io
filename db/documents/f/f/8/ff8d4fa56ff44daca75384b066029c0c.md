CRC affects approximately 135,439 estimated new patients with a corresponding 95,520 (70%) due to Cca in the United States every year. Among all cancers sites, it is the second leading cause of death in the United States, with an estimated 50,260 deaths, when colon and rectum are combined. The CRC incidence rate has been declining 3% per year since 2004 although increasing by 2% per year among screened young adults (younger than 50 years).
