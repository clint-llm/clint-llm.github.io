The interprofessional healthcare team should be familiar with the drug's indications and contraindications, including the primary care providers, physician assistants, and nurse practitioners who prescribe zileuton. Zileuton is only used for prophylaxis of asthma and is not recommended for monotherapy. It also has several off-label uses. Clinicians should monitor the patient's liver function on zileuton regularly. Nurses can monitor for any neuropsychiatric symptoms and report to the clinician team.****According to CDC, moderate to severe asthma is a risk factor for severe COVID-19 infection.

Pulmonologist consultation may be required if asthma is not controlled despite optimal medical therapy. The Asthma Quality of Life Questionnaire (AQLQ)(American thoracic society) can assess health-related quality of life.
