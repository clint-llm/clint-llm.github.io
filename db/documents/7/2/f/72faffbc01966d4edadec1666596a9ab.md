In the majority of cases, dermoid cysts occur in the head and neck region, although they may be found anywhere on the body.

A lower lid dermoid cyst may be evident as a painless, gradually enlarging swelling of the lower lid.

Dermoid cysts usually occur as solitary lesions; however, multiple concurrent dermoid cysts have also been reported.

Hair protruding from a dermoid cyst punctum is pathognomic for dermoid cysts.
