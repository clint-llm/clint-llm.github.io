Common adverse effects include:

- Nausea

- Diarrhea

- Headache

- Dizziness

- Sedation

- Dry mouth

- Rash

Rare yet clinically fatal adverse effects include

- Hemolytic anemia (Coombs positive)

- Lupus-like syndrome

- Myocarditis

- Pancreatitis

- Hepatotoxicity

- Immune thrombocytopenia

- Reversible leukopenia

- Involuntary choreoathetotic movements

- Weight gain

- Rebound hypertension
