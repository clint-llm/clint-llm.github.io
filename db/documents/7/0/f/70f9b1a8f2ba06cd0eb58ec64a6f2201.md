An Addisonian crisis, or adrenal crisis, is a potentially life-threatening condition that results from an acute insufficiency of adrenal hormones (glucocorticoid or mineralocorticoid) and requires immediate treatment. This activity reviews the evaluation and management of the Addisonian crisis and highlights the importance of the interprofessional team in recognizing and managing this condition.

**Objectives:**
- Explain how a patient with an Addisonian crisis might present.
- Explain the evaluation of a patient with a probable Addisonian crisis.
- Describe the treatment of an Addisonian crisis. 
- Identify interprofessional team strategies to improve care coordination and improve outcomes for patients with Addisonian crisis.
