The following complications can occur with acetabular fractures

- Post-traumatic arthritis and osteonecrosis; quality of reduction is the main determinant for risk of late arthritis and goal should be a reduction within 1 mm

- Infection (approx 5%)

- Iatrogenic nerve injury

- DVT(4% symptomatic DVT, 1% PE)

- Intra-articular hardware

- Heterotopic ossification (extended IF more than KL more than ilioinguinal)
