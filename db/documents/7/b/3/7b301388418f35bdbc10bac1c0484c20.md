Pleural effusion is a frequent complication of advanced malignancy with significant associated morbidity and mortality. Recent advances in the management of malignant pleural effusions (MPE) have changed the morbidity associated with this disease.

**Objectives:**
- Describe the pathophysiology of malignant pleural effusions.
- Review the evaluation of a pleural effusion.
- Summarize the treatment options for malignant pleural effusion.
- Outline the importance of improving care coordination among interprofessional team members to improve outcomes for patients affected by malignant pleural effusion.
