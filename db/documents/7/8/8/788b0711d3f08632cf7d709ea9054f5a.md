**Serious Reactions**

- Suicidality

- Depression

- Steven-Johnson syndrome

- Anaphylaxis

- Angioedema

- Erythema multiforme

- Rhabdomyolysis

- Withdrawal seizure or withdrawal symptoms if discontinued abruptly

**More Common Reactions**

- Ataxia

- Dizziness

- Fatigue

- Somnolence

- Fever

- Nystagmus

- Peripheral edema

- Hostility and hyperkinesia (pediatric)

- Nausea and vomiting

- Tremor

- Asthenia

- Diplopia

- Diarrhea

- Xerostomia

- Infection

- Amblyopia

- Headache

- Constipation

- Weight gain

- Abnormal thinking

- Amnesia

- Back pain

- Impotence

- Depression
