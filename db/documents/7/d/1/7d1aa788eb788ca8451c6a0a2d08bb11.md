Preterm labor is labor occurring between after 20 and before 37 weeks gestation. Preterm labors are subcategorized as early or late preterm. Early preterm labor occurs prior to 33 weeks gestation, and late preterm labor occurs between 34 and 36 weeks gestation.

**Objectives:**
- Describe the types of preterm labor. 
- Review the common presentations of preterm labor. 
- Summarize optimal management strategies for preterm labor. 
- Explain the role of collaboration among interprofessional team members to improve care coordination and minimize oversight, leading to earlier recognition of preterm labor and better management and outcomes.
