The nursing and the allied health staff help take the patient to the operating room, arrange the equipment and instrument trolley, drape the patients, and assist in surgical intervention. They also help in patient examination preoperatively as well as postoperatively. Also, assist in regular monitoring, counseling, and follow-up of these patients. The mid-level ophthalmic personnel helps in patient recruitment, assistance in the outpatient department, corneal scraping, and explaining the procedure and time needed for the scraping results to arrive.
