Leflunomide is available in oral tablets and comes in the following dosages:

- 10 mg

- 20 mg

- 100 mg

Patients can be started on an oral loading dose of 100 mg once a day for three days, followed by a maintenance dose of 10 mg or 20 mg per day -depending on the patient's tolerance. These are the recommended dosages for the treatment of Rheumatoid arthritis.
