Mandible is one of the most commonly fractured facial bone and is frequently seen in the emergency department. This activity reviews the anatomy of the mandible, the diagnosis and the management of mandibular fracture. This activity highlights the role of the interprofessional team in caring for patients affected by mandibular fractures.

**Objectives:**
- Describe the anatomy of mandible.
- Describe the pathophysiology of mandibular fracture.
- Describe the diagnostic tool for the evaluation of mandibular fracture.
- Describe the treatment of mandibular fracture.
