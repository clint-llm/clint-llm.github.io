Symptoms and signs of acute cholangitis mimic other diseases of the liver and intestine. Following differentials should be ruled out in these patients:

- Acute cholecystitis

- Hepatitis

- Liver cirrhosis

- Liver failure

- Hepatic abscess

- Pancreatitis

- Perforated peptic ulcer

- Acute appendicitis

- Diverticulitis

- Pyelonephritis

- Mesenteric ischemia

- Septic shock
