Acute upper respiratory tract infections include rhinitis, pharyngitis, tonsillitis, and laryngitis. Symptoms of URTIs commonly include:

- Cough

- Sore throat

- Runny nose

- Nasal congestion

- Headache

- Low-grade fever

- Facial pressure

- Sneezing

- Malaise

- Myalgias
