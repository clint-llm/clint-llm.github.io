There are numerous complications that can occur depending on the specific etiology, particularly in those with systemic illnesses. The following is a list of a few well-known complications in those with nonidiopathic interstitial pulmonary fibrosis:

- Acute and/or chronic respiratory failure

- Irreversible pulmonary fibrosis

- Pulmonary hypertension

- Pulmonary arterial hypertension in those with systemic sclerosis

- Cor pulmonale

- Thromboembolic disease

- Malignancy

- Spontaneous pneumothorax
