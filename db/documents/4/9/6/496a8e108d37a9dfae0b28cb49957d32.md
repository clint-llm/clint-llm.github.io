**Absolute Contraindications**

The pharmacological vasodilators are contraindicated in the following cases

- Obstructive lung disease with ongoing wheezing or a history of reactive airway disease

- Second- or third-degree AV block without a pacemaker

- Sinus node disease without a pacemaker

- Systolic BP < 90 mmHg, especially in the following scenarios:

- 
              
- Autonomic dysfunction
              
              
- Hypovolemia
              
              
- Left main coronary artery stenosis
              
              
- Stenotic valvular heart disease
              
              
- Pericarditis or pericardial effusions
              
              
- Stenotic carotid artery disease with cerebrovascular insufficiency

- Uncontrolled hypertension (systolic BP greater than 200 mmHg or diastolic BP greater than 110 mmHg)

- Recent (less than48 hours) use of dipyridamole or acetylsalicylic acid/dipyridamole

- Known hypersensitivity to pharmacological vasodilators

- Acute coronary syndromes

- Recent (2 to 4 days) after acute myocardial infarction

**Relative Contraindications**

- Sinus bradycardia with heart rates less than 40 beats per minute

- Mobitz Type 1 second-degree AV block

- Ingestion of caffeinated foods/beverages within the last 12 hours

- Severe aortic stenosis

- Seizure disorder
