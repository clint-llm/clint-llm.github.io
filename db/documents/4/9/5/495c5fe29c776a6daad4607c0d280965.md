All HBO2 treatments must be directly supervised by a physician trained in hyperbaric medicine. Physicians that have the minimum required 40-hour introductory course training should be precepted by physician Board Certified in UHM or have a certificate of additional training (CAQ) in UHM. Support personnel includes nursing staff, preferably a certified hyperbaric nurse (CHRN), and certified hyperbaric technicians (CHTs).
