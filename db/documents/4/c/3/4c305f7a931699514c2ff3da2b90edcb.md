Positive ANCA lab results with clinical suspicion aids in diagnosing some small vessel vasculitis, including pauci-immune small vessel vasculitides granulomatosis with polyangiitis (GPA or Wegener granulomatosis), microscopic polyangiitis (MPA), eosinophilic granulomatosis with polyangiitis (EGPA or Churg-Strauss syndrome) and anti-glomerular basement disease.
