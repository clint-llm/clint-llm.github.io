**Common Adverse Effects**

- Dry mouth

- Flatulence

- Abdominal cramps

- Nausea

- Ileus

- Constipation

- Urinary retention

- Dizziness

- Drowsiness

**Serious Adverse Effects**

- Toxic megacolon

- Necrotizing enterocolitis

- Stevens-Johnson syndrome

- Toxic epidermal necrolysis

- Syncope

- QT/QTc interval prolongation, torsades de pointed, ventricular tachycardia

- Other ventricular arrhythmias and/or cardiac arrest

**Drug Interactions**

It is extracted in the liver and metabolized mainly by CYP3A4, a cytochrome P450 enzyme.
