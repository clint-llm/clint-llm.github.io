Patients should be instructed to keep track of the diet they eat as well as the amount of water they drink daily. Diet rich in phosphate should be encouraged, which includes dairy products, meat, poultry, and peanuts. They should follow up with the physician as advised or if any of these problems arise:

- Confusion

- Irritable behavior

- Pain in muscles

- Nausea, vomiting, or diarrhea

- Constipation that lasts longer than 2 days
