Bisphosphonates are very efficacious in the prevention of fractures in patients with osteoporosis. They are relatively safe, although they do have correlations with some rare and serious adverse effects. Patient education is crucial to ensure compliance and effective treatment of osteoporosis. Patients shall receive education to eliminate other risk factors associated with osteoporotic fractures, including smoking cessation, weight-bearing exercises, avoiding excessive alcohol intake, and increasing dairy product intake.
