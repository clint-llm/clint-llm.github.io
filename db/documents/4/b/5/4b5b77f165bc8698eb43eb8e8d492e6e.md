**Extra-thyroidal Extension**

Extra-thyroidal extension occurs in 8% to 32% of the cases.

**Local or Regional Recurrences**

Local or regional recurrences occur in 5 to 15% of patients with PTC.

**Distant Metastases**

Distant metastasis occurs in only 1% to 25%, mainly in the lungs and bones. Other less common sites are the brain, liver, and skin.
