- Familial hypercholesterolemia - Overlapping clinical features with sitosterolemia but features very high LDL levels

- Lecithin-cholesterol acyltransferase (LCAT) deficiency - Features very low HDL, elevated very-low-density lipoprotein (VLDL), and hypertriglyceridemia

- Tangier disease - HDL levels are severely low, which is not seen in sitosterolemia
