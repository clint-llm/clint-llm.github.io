Patient restraint and seclusion is a controversial yet necessary aspect of medicine. Restraint encompasses both physical and chemical procedures. This activity outlines the proper application of patient restraint and seclusion and highlights the role of the interprofessional healthcare team in evaluating and managing a restrained patient.

**Objectives:**
- Identify the function and key concepts of patient restraint and seclusion.
- Describe the issues of concern in regards to patient restraint and seclusion.
- Outline the clinical significance of patient restraint and seclusion.
