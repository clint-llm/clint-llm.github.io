- If lactic acidosis is drug induced, discontinue the drug

- Start 2 large bore IVs for fluid resuscitation

- Administer oxygen if saturation is less than 94

- Monitor ins and outs

- Place patient on cardiac monitor

- Check labs to ensure patient lactate levels are dropping

- Check Chest x-ray report to see if patient has heart failure

- If patientis diabetic, cover patient blood glucose with sliding scale regular insulin

- Check peripheral pulses

- Palpate abdomen to ensure patient has no abdominal cause like mesenteric ischemia
