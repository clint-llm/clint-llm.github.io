Proper sleep is vital to maintain health and optimize overall functioning during periods of wakefulness. Sleeplessness has been linked with several health problems such as diabetes, obesity, and heart disease. It is also linked to decreased work performance, traffic accidents, mood and relationship problems, and countless other issues. This activity will focus on the diagnosis and management of circadian rhythm sleep-wake disorders and explains the role of the interprofessional team in managing those with sleeplessness and circadian disorder.

**Objectives:**
- Identify the most common etiologies of sleep disorders.
- Outline the evaluation for each of the common sleep disorders.
- Summarize the common sleep disorders with the appropriate treatment recommendations.
- Review the importance of collaboration and communication among the interprofessional team in diagnosing and managing the different sleep disorders to improve the quality of life for those with this condition.
