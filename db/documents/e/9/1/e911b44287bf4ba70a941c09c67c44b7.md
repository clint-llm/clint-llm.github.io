Contraindications for MET of the lumbar spine include recent surgery of the lumbar region, significant injury of the lumbar spine including lumbar spinal fracture or acute cord compression, cauda equina syndrome, lumbar spinal malignancy, metastasis of primary malignancy to the lumbar spine, osteomyelitis of the lumbar spine, severe osteoporosis, connective tissue disease, and rheumatoid arthritis.
