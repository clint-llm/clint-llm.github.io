When treating patients with thermal burns, the major surgical procedures will be debridement/excision, grafting, and reconstruction. This activity reviews burn care and highlights the role of the healthcare team in treating patients who undergo burn surgery.

**Objectives:**
- Outline the presentation of a patient with burn injuries.
- Describe the treatment considerations for patients with burn injuries.
- Summarize the management considerations for patients with burn injuries.
- Explain the importance of collaboration and communication amongst the interdisciplinary team to improve outcomes for patients affected by burns.
