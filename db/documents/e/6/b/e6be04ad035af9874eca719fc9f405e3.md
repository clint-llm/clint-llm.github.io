The various histopathological features in PBK are loss of corneal endothelial cells, pleomorphism and polymegathism of endothelial cells, reduced stromal keratocytes, thickening of posterior collagen layer of Descemet membrane, lack of adhesive proteins like laminin, fibronectin, and type 4 collagen in the epithelial basement membrane, increased antiadhesive proteins like tenascin C and thrombospondin-1 and increases levels of interleukin 8 (IL-8), interleukin 2 (IL-2), insulin-like growth factor (IGF-1), bone marrow factor-4 (BMP-4), and transforming growth factor (TGF- Î²).
