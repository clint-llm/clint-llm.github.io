After reviewing this article, participants should understand how to diagnose, treat, and post-treatment monitoring. This article reviews the natural history, epidemiology, clinical presentation, differential diagnosis, evaluation, treatment, complications, and prognosis of squamous cell cervical cancer. It also outlines the importance of patient education and health care teamwork in enhancing cervical cancer management outcomes.

**Objectives:**
- Describe the pathologic processes that can lead to the development of cervical cancer.
- Explain the differential diagnosis of cervical cancer.
- Outline the treatment options available for the management of cervical cancer.
- Discuss interprofessional team strategies for improving care coordination and communication for effective management of cervical cancer.
