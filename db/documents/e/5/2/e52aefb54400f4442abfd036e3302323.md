Indications for ablative laser resurfacing include:

- Photoaging

- Facial wrinkles

- Acne scars

- Surgical or traumatic scars

- Actinic keratoses

- Seborrheic keratoses

- Warts

- Moles and other nevi

- Xanthelasma

- Skin tags

- Rhinophyma

- Sebaceous hyperplasia

- Pyogenic granuloma

- Neurofibroma

- Angiofibroma

- Actinic cheilitis

- Keloids
