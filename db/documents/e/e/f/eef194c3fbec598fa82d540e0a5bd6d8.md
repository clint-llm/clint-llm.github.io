CAD is the leading cause of death in adults in the United States. The 2016 Heart Disease and Stroke Statistics update of the American Heart Association (AHA) reported that there are about 15.5 million adults in the United States who have CAD, and the overall death rate from CAD was 102.6 per 100,000. There is one myocardial infarction every 42 seconds. Age is an independent risk factor for CAD. In the United States, the lifetime risk of developing CAD with at least two major risk factors is 37.5% for men and 18.3% for women.
