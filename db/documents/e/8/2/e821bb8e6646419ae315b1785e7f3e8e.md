This article reviews the various types of upper eyelid reconstruction that are used to repair lid defects, the principles that govern which approaches clinicians to prefer for certain types of eyelid defects, the potential complications, and the importance of an interprofessional approach for repairing defects created after surgical resection of malignancies.

**Objectives:**
- Explain the functional, cosmetic, and anatomic principles that determine reconstructive surgery of the upper eyelid.
- Describe the type of reconstruction based on the location and extent of the defect.
- Summarize the outcomes after eyelid reconstruction. 
- Outline the importance of an interprofessional approach to repair lid defects that arise from malignancies.
