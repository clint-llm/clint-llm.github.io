Patients with active tuberculosis undergoing medical treatment should undergo organized monitoring and follow-up. Sputum sampling and culture with drug susceptibility testing should be done at the third and fourth months into treatment to verify negativity. A sample should also be obtained at the end of treatment. Chest X-rays or equivalent imaging should be obtained two months after treatment initiation and at the end of therapy. Vision assessment should be done monthly, starting the third month till the end of therapy. Screening for hepatitis B and C should be done at baseline for patients at risk (such as IV drug users).
