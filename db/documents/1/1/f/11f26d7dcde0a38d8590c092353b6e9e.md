The pulmonary rehabilitation program can be conducted in the hospital, outpatient, or home. The ability to provide rehabilitation in different settings can help assist patients with varying severity of pulmonary impairments. Equipment for exercise and monitoring are essential components of the program, and they vary depending on the location and setting. Exercise equipment usually includes a treadmill, stationary bicycle, access to stairs or step box, arm crank ergometer, hand weights, resistive exercise bands, and weights.
