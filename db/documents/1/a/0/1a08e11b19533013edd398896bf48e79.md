Antineutrophilic cytoplasmic antibody (ANCA) associated vasculitides are rare diseases. The incidence reported being 10 to 20 cases per million. GPA is the most common disease of the three types, with an incidence of 5 to 10 cases per million and a peak incidence in middle age (approximately 55 years). MPA is less common than GPA with a male to female ratio of 2:1, and EGPA is the rarest of all.
