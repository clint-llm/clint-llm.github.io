**Intrinsic Muscles of the Larynx that Act on the Vocal Cords**

- Tensors: Cricothyroid, vocalis

- Abductor: Posterior cricoarytenoid muscles

- Adductors: Lateral cricoarytenoid, transverse arytenoid

**Intrinsic Muscles of the Larynx that Act on the Laryngeal Inlet**

- Openers: Thyroepiglottic

- Closers: Aryepiglottic, oblique arytenoid

**Extrinsic Muscles of the Larynx**

- Elevators: Stylopharyngeus, salpingopharyngeus, palatopharyngeus, thyrohyoid

- Depressors: Sternohyoid, sternothyroid, omohyoid
