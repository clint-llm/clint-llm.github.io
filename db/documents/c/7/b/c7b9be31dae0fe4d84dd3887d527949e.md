The differential diagnosis of tricuspid regurgitation are as follows:

- Ascites

- Cirrhosis

- Ebstein anomaly

- Eisenmenger syndrome

- Heart failure

- Marfan syndrome

- Cardiogenic shock

- Atrial fibrillation

- Dilated cardiomyopathy

- Biliary disease

- Cor pulmonale

- Mitral regurgitation

- Carcinoid tumor
