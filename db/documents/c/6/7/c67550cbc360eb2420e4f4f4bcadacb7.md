Patients present with radial-sided wrist pain which is typically worsened by thumb and wrist motion. The condition may be associated with pain or difficulty with tasks such as opening a jar lid. Tenderness overlying the radial styloid is usually present, and fusiform swelling in this region may also be appreciated.
