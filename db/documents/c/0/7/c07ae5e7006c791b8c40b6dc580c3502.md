**Common Adverse Reactions**

- Peripheral neuropathy

- Pancreatitis

- Portal hypertension

- Lactic acidosis

- Hepatitis

- Optical neuritis

- Hepatomegaly with steatosis

- Hyperglycemia

- Development of diabetes mellitus

- Hyperuricemia

- Depression

- Restlessness

- Anxiety

**Uncommon Severe Adverse Reactions**

- Diarrhea

- Headache

- Nausea

- Rash

- Vomiting

- Agitation

- Dizziness
