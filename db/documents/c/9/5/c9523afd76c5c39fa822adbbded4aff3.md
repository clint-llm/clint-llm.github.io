**Epiphyseal Lesions**

- Chondroblastoma (10 to 25 years)

- Giant cell tumor ( 20 to 40 years)

- Chondrosarcoma (rare)

**Diaphyseal Lesions**

- Ewing sarcoma (5 to 25 years)

- Lymphoma

- Fibrous dysplasia (5 to 30 years)

- Adamantinoma (in tibia)

- Histiocytosis ( 5 to 30 years)

**Multiple Lesions**

- Histiocytosis

- Enchondroma

- Osteochondroma

- Fibrous dysplasia

- Metastasis

- Multiple myelomas

- Infection

- Hyperparathyroidism

- Hemangioma
