Complications of cryptogenic cirrhosis include the same complications one would find with other causes of cirrhosis. Additional potential complications for cryptogenic cirrhosis include heart disease, renal disease and other potential complications of diabetes such as retinopathy. Additional complications depend on the underlying etiology of cryptogenic cirrhosis.
