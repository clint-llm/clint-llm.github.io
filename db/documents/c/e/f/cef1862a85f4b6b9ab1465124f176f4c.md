Numerous conditions and medications can lead to PSVT in patients.

Some of these conditions and medications are listed below:

- Hyperthyroidism

- Caffeinated beverages

- Nicotine

- Hydralazine

- Atropine

- Adenosine

- Verapamil

- Salbutamol

- Ecstasy

- Cocaine

- Amphetamines

- Alcohol

- Digoxin toxicity

- Myocardial infarction

- Structural heart disease

- Ebstein anomaly

- Pericarditis

- Myocarditis

- Cardiomyopathy

- Pulmonary embolism

- Rheumatic heart disease

- Mitral valve prolapse

- Pneumonia

- Chronic lung disease

- Chest wall trauma

- Anxiety

- Hypovolemia

- Hypoxia
