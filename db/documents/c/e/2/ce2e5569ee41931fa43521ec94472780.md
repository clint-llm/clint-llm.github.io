Sarcoidosis is a multisystem disorder of unknown etiology that mostly affects young adults worldwide and presents with noncaseating granulomas in various organs. Characteristically it presents with bilateral hilar lymphadenopathy and reticular opacities in the lungs. Other major involved sites include skin, eyes, and joints, although it can express to a variable degree in the musculoskeletal system, reticuloendothelial system, exocrine glands, heart, kidney, and central nervous system.
