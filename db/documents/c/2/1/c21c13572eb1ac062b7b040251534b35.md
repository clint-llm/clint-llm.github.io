This activity describes the current status of abdominal hysterectomy, which represents the most common gynecologic surgical procedure performed in the United States. It will highlight the most common indications, will review surgical steps, and describe the role of the inter[professional team in the evaluation and management of the patient undergoing abdominal hysterectomy.

**Objectives:**
- Identify the anatomical structures considered landmarks for abdominal hysterectomy.
- Describe the surgical steps of the abdominal hysterectomy.
- Identify the most common indications of abdominal hysterectomy.
