Treatment for calcitriol overdose includes general supportive measures. Immediate discontinuation of calcitriol and a low calcium diet is recommended. In cases of persistent, elevated calcium levels, drugs such as phosphates, corticosteroids, and thiazide diuretics may help induce excretion and forced diuresis.
