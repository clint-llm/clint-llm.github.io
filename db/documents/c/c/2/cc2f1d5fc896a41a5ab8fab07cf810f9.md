The causes of hypovolemia are broadly divided into renal and extrarenal etiologies.

**Renal**

- Diuretic excess

- Mineralocorticoid deficiency

- Ketonuria

- Osmotic diuresis

- Cerebral salt wasting syndrome

- Salt-wasting nephropathies

**Extrarenal**

- Vomiting

- Diarrhea

- Third spacing of fluid

- Burns

- Pancreatitis

- Trauma

- Bleeding
