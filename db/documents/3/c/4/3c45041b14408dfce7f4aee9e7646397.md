The differential diagnosis of cyanosis also includes:

- Anemia

- Asthma

- Congestive heart failure

- Cyanotic congenital heart disease

- Peripheral cyanosis

- Polycythemia

- Sulfhemoglobinemia

The differential diagnosis of bluish skin discoloration also includes:

- Acrodermatitis enteropathica

- Amiodarone-induced skin pigmentation

- Argyria
