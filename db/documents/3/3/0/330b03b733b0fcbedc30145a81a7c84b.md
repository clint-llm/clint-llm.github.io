Effective sexual harassment strategies start with top leadership and involve every team member. Maintaining an environment of inclusion and respect requires commitment from all staff, regardless of their hierarchical levels. Coordinated interprofessional management and an integrated strategic approach are necessary to avoid these unprofessional and unwanted behaviors.

In healthcare, threatening situations can come from patients, peers, and superiors. Teamwork is the best policy. Creating alliances among female coworkers and encouraging superiors to intervene and act as mediators for those affected are beneficial practices.
