The indications of MSICS depend on the surgeon's experience, skill, and expertise in managing the cases.

**For Beginners**

- Nuclear sclerosis grade 2-3

- Posterior subcapsular cataract

- Cortical cataracts

**For Experienced Surgeons**

- Nuclear sclerosis grade 4

- Nuclear sclerosis grade 5

- Hard mature cataract

- Brown cataract

- Intumescent cataract

- Morgagnian cataract

- Traumatic cataract

- Temporal SICS in glaucoma cases

- Posterior polar cataract

- Complicated cataract

- Pseudoexfoliation

- Subluxated cataract

- Dislocated cataract

- Small pupil cases

- Phacolytic glaucoma

- Phacomorphic glaucoma

- High myopia

- High axial length

- Deep socket

- Nanophthalmos

- Pediatric cataract

- IOL exchange

- Secondary IOL cases

- Post vitrectomy
