The differential diagnoses for anabolic steroid toxicity are broad as AAS's affect many organ systems.

- Adrenal neoplasm

- Anorexia

- Cirrhosis

- Conn syndrome

- Drug-induced hirsutism

- Drug-induced jaundice

- Hemolytic anemia

- Hepatic malignancy

- Hepatitis

- Ovarian cancer

- Precocious puberty

- Thromboembolic state

- Vasculopathy

- Viral myocarditis
