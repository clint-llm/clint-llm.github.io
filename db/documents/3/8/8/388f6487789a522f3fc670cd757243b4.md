The majority of complications are associated with P. falciparum and include the following:

- Pulmonary edema

- Cerebral malaria

- Renal dysfunction

- Anemia

- Death

- Seizures

- Hypoglycemia

- Hemoglobinuria

- Lactic acidosis

- Thrombocytopenia

- Splenic rupture

The majority of deaths occur in children less than five years of age.
