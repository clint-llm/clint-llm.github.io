Avoiding mosquito bites and enforcing vector control measures is crucial to avoid contracting the disease. Tourist should avoid visiting endemic areas. Visitors touring endemic areas should sleep under mosquito nets, wear long sleeves and trousers, and use mosquito repellent on exposed parts of the body. There is currently no vaccine available. Individuals with lymphedema should maintain good personal hygiene and wash affected areas with antiseptic solution daily. Compression stockings, regular exercise, and using a pillow beneath the affected limb at night can help in reducing the swelling.
