The equipment required for radial artery bypass graft include:

- Operation theatre

- Cardiopulmonary machine

- Surgical drape

- Sternal saw

- Clamps

- Retractors

- Forceps

- Scissors

- Needle holder

- Dilators and dissectors

- Suction cannula

- Sutures and Needles

- Arterial cannula

- Venous cannula

- Cardioplegia cannula
