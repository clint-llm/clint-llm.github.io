The most common clinical manifestations of adrenal crisis include: weakness, severe fatigue, unintentional weight loss, nausea, vomiting, abdominal pain, decreased appetite, back or limb pain, dizziness, somnolence, confusion, and loss of consciousness

On the assessment of vitals, these patients can have fever, tachycardia, and hypotension (including orthostatic)

A review of the patient's past medical history and past surgical history is mandatory. The clinicians should review the list of home medications and supplements.
