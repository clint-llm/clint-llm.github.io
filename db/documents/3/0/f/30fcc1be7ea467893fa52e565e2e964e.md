If retinoblastoma is left untreated patient is likely to develop the following complications:

- Retinal detachment

- Retinal necrosis

- Orbital invasion

- Optic nerve invasion

- Blindness

- Intracranial extension

- Secondary neoplasms

- Metastasis

- Tumor recurrence

- Temporal bone hypoplasia

- Cataract

- Radiation neuropathy

- Radiation retinopathy
