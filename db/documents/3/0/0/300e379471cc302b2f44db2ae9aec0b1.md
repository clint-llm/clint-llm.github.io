Liver biopsy remains the gold standard in the diagnosis of several liver disorders. With refinement in techniques, it has emerged as a safe, useful tool for hepatologists managing the gamut of liver pathologies. This activity describes the indications, contraindications of liver biopsy and highlights the role of the interprofessional team in the preoperative workup of these patients.

**Objectives:**
- Describe the technique of a liver biopsy.
- Review the indications for a liver biopsy.
- Summarize the complications of a liver biopsy.
- Explain the importance of improving care coordination among the interprofessional team to enhance the delivery of care for patients undergoing a liver biopsy.
