Rheumatoid arthritis (RA) is an immune-mediated multisystem inflammatory disease that predominantly affects the synovial joints. This activity provides a brief review of rheumatoid arthritis, with a focus on rheumatoid arthritis of the hands as regards the presentation, evaluation, and management and highlights the role of the interprofessional team in the diagnosis and management of RA of hand.

**Objectives:**
- Review the etiologic factors in rheumatoid arthritis.
- Review disease features in rheumatoid arthritis.
- Outline the management of rheumatoid arthritis.
- Outline latest updates in patient handling, evaluation and treatment.
