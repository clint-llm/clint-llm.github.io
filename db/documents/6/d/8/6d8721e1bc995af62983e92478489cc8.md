The exact pathogenesis of Plummer-Vinson syndrome and the formation of the esophageal web is not well known. It has been postulated that iron deficiency induces iron-dependent enzyme dysfunction, leading to oxidative stress and DNA damage.

On biopsy, no inflammatory infiltrates are found.

A complicated inlet patch, heterotopic gastric mucosa, has also been proposed in the pathogenesis of PVS.
