Thyroid eye disease cases can present with the following complications:

- Dysthyroid optic neuropathy - compressive or ischemic

- Optic atrophy

- Exposure-keratopathy and keratomalacia

- Open-angle glaucoma

- Restrictive strabismus and diplopia

- Persistent proptosis, lid retraction, cosmetic disfigurement

- Orbital venous stasis and venous occlusions
