As CBT with exposure and response prevention are the mainstay of non-pharmacological treatment, it is essential to consult a therapist well versed in this therapy.

Medical consultants depend on the severity and type of compulsion. For example, if hand washing is excessive, dermatitis may evolve, and the patient may need dermatological help. Treat the whole patient, not just the psychological symptoms

It is important to coordinate care with the pediatrician and/or family doctor as medication side effects such as weight gain and tics should be monitored.
