Pityriasis versicolor may be confused with various conditions:

- Pityriasis rosea

- Tinea corporis

- Vitiligo

- Pityriasis alba

- Confluent reticulated papillomatosis of Gougerot and Carteaud

- Postinflammatory hypo- and hyperpigmentation

- Seborrheic dermatitis

- Guttate psoriasis

- Tinea corporis

- Nummular eczema

- Secondary syphilis

- Mycosis fungoides
