Several landmark trials were done over the past few decades which have totally changed the way we care for coronary artery disease patients. It is beyond the scope of this article to discuss individual trial results, however, the following are the names of some important studies. ISIS-2, CURE, CLARITY-TIMI 28, TIRTON-TIMI 38, PLATO, and CURRENT-OASIS 7 trials were done regarding guidelines for antiplatelet medications.
