These patients can present to the emergency department, urgent care, or to their primary care provider for initial injury evaluation. It is of utmost importance that the recognition of these injuries is appreciated. The diagnosis is missed on initial clinical and radiographic evaluation in up to 25% of cases. Early recognition and diagnosis of these injuries are prudent to restore patient function and prevent morbidity.
