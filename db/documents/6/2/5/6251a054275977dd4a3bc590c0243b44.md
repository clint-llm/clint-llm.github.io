The various equipment and instruments needed to perform CXL are

- Conjunctival forceps

- Diluted absolute alcohol

- Calliper

- Ink pen marker

- Hockey stick blade

- Crescent blade

- Riboflavin

- Ultraviolet light

- Hand-held slit lamp

- Normal saline

- Saline cannula

In the case of accelerated CXL, an accelerated CXL machine is needed.
