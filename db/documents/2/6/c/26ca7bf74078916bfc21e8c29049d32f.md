Magnesium is a mineral that is naturally found in your body and in the food you consume daily. It is responsible for many processes within the body that include, but are not limited to, protein synthesis, bone formation, blood pressure regulation, nerve function, and electrical conduction in the heart. Additionally, it will highlight the importance of diagnosing and treating magnesium toxicity early on and how a strong interprofessional effort is important to prevent fatal complications.
