Differential diagnosis includes other hemolytic anemias, other causes of atypical thrombosis, and conditions that cause bone marrow failure. Differential diagnosis considerations should include conditions such as paroxysmal cold hemoglobinuria, autoimmune hemolytic anemia, primary BM disorders such as aplastic anemia, myelodysplastic syndrome, primary myelofibrosis, microangiopathic hemolytic anemia, disseminated intravascular coagulation, and other hereditary anemias.
