Surgical repair of entropion is performed to restore the eyelid to its proper anatomical alignment. This, in turn, protects the eye from injury and reduces associated symptoms.

Perioperative medical management is vital to prevent further corneal damage. This can include ocular lubrication with ointments, artificial tears, or ocular protection with contact lenses.
