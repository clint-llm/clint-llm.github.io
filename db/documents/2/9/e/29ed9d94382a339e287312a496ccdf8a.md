On chest X-ray posteroanterior (PA) view, the pulmonary disease is divided into five stages:

- Stage 0: Unremarkable chest X-ray

- Stage I: Enlargement of the hilar or mediastinal lymph nodes

- Stage II: Parenchymal disease along with enlargement of the lymph nodes

- Stage III: Only parenchymal disease

- Stage IV: Pulmonary fibrosis or end-stage lung disease
