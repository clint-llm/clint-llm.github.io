While assessing a patient with osteosarcoma following differentials should be considered:

- Other types of tumor (both malignant and benign):
- Osseous metastasis from other types of malignancies
- Ewing sarcoma
- Malignant fibrous histiocytoma
- Fibrosarcoma
- Giant cell tumors
- Lymphoma
- Osteoblastoma
- Cortical desmoid

- Non-tumor bone lesions:
- Osteomyelitis
- Aneurysmal bone cyst
- Fibrous dysplasia
