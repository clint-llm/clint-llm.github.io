Monitoring for signs of drug misuse of MOR agonists is a very important responsibility for the healthcare team because of the epidemic rates of drug misuse worldwide, e.g., the USA, which lead to death because of respiratory depression. Methods for monitoring drug abuse as well as drug diversion include the following examples: assessment surveys, state prescription drug monitoring programs, urine screening, adherence check-lists, motivational counseling, and dosage form counting, e.g., tablet counting.
