Complications include contractions of respiratory muscles, vocal cords, and other critical areas of the body.

- Autonomic dysfunction- leading to hypertension

- Asphyxia

- Long bone fractures

- Paralytic ileus

- Joint dislocation

- Aspiration pneumonia

- Pressure sores

- Stress ulcers

- Coma

- Nerve palsy

- Urine retention

- Seizures
