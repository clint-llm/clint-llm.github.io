Bag valve mask ventilation is a difficult skill to master. This activity explains how to improve this skill. This activity describes the technique of bag-mask ventilation, its indications, and highlights the role of the interprofessional team in the management of patients in respiratory distress.

**Objectives:**
- Describe the anatomy of the airway.
- Summarize the indications for bag valve mask ventilation.
- Review the technique of bag valve mask ventilation.
- Outline the complexities of bag-valve ventilation in critically ill patients so that outcomes can be improved.
