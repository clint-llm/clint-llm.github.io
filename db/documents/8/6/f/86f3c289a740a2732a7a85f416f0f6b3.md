Skin biopsy is an important and common diagnostic procedure in dermatology practice. Various methods of skin biopsy exist, and multiple factors are considered in choosing the most appropriate biopsy technique for a given situation. This activity describes the methods and concepts in performing and choosing specific biopsy procedures.

**Objectives:**
- Identify the anatomical structures, indications, and contraindications of different types of skin biopsies.
- Describe the equipment, personnel, preparation, and technique in regard to skin biopsy.
- Outline the potential complications and clinical significance of skin biopsy.
- Summarize interprofessional team strategies for improving care coordination and communication to advance skin biopsy expertise and improve outcomes.
