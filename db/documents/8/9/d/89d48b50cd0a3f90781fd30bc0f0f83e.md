Minimally invasive glaucoma surgery may be considered in the following:

- Mild to moderate glaucoma cases

- Primary open angle glaucoma (POAG), pigmentary glaucoma and pseudoexfoliation glaucoma (PXFG)

- Intraocular pressure is not reduced even after maximal antiglaucoma therapy and laser trabeculoplasty

- Noncompliance to treatment

- Adverse drug reaction
