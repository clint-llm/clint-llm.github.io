The endoscopic brow lift aims to restore a more youthful and rested appearance to the upper third of the face. This activity reviews the evaluation of brow ptosis and the technical aspects of endoscopic brow lifting and describes the role of the interprofessional team in improving care for patients who undergo endoscopic brow lifting.

**Objectives:**
- Identify the anatomical structures involved in an endoscopic brow lift.
- Describe the equipment, personnel, preparation, and technique of endoscopic brow lifting.
- Review the potential complications associated with performing an endoscopic brow lift.
- Outline interprofessional team strategies for improving care coordination and communication to improve outcomes from the endoscopic brow lift procedure.
