Detrusor sphincter dyssynergia is a common cause of lower urinary tract symptoms in a patient with spinal cord injury and various other neurological diseases. Because a myriad of other clinical conditions present with similar symptoms, this condition should be promptly diagnosed, and treatment initiated early in the disease course. This activity reviews the evaluation and treatment of detrusor sphincter dyssynergia and highlights the holistic approach in the care of patients with this condition.

**Objectives:**
- Describe the pathophysiology of detrusor sphincter dyssynergia.
- Review the appropriate evaluation of a patient with detrusor sphincter dyssynergia.
- Summarize different treatment modalities and their complications in patients with detrusor sphincter dyssynergia.
- Explain interprofessional team strategies for improving care coordination and communication to advance the care of detrusor sphincter dyssynergia and improve outcomes.
