Cyclic neutropenia is a very rare hematological condition characterized by regular fluctuations in blood neutrophil counts, leading to periodic neutropenia with a 21-day turnover frequency. This activity reviews the evaluation and management of cyclic neutropenia and highlights the role of the interprofessional team in managing patients with this condition.

**Objectives:**
- Identify the etiology of cyclic neutropenia medical conditions and emergencies.
- Outline and explain the appropriate history, physical, and evaluation of cyclic neutropenia.
- Review the treatment and management options available for cyclic neutropenia.
- Summarize interprofessional team strategies for improving care coordination and communication to advance the management of cyclic neutropenia and improve outcomes.
