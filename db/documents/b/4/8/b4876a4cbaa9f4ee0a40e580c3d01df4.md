Multiple theories leading to a contracted socket.

**Trauma and Surgery-related**

- Fibrosis following the initial trauma

- Extensive orbital dissection

- Poor surgical techniques (evisceration/ enucleation)

- Multiple socket procedures

**Ocular Surface-related**

- A cicatrizing disease of the conjunctiva

- Sever socket infection and inflammation

- Keloid formation in the socket

- Poor vascular supply

**Prosthesis and Implant-related**

- Non-wearing of prothesis

- Faulty/ill-fitting prosthesis

- Implant migration / exposure

**Etiology Related**

- Alkali burns

- Irradiation following enucleation in some retinoblastoma
