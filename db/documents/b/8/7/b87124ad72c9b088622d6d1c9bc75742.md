**Age-related Travel in Lashes**

Studies of lashes in different age groups have shown that there is a generalized reduction in the thickness, pigmentation, and length of lashes with age but there are few proper longitudinal studies. Thibault improved our understanding of lashes and the change over time by studying defined lashes over nine months, measuring the length each week.

**Canities**is the natural gradual graying of hair shafts because of tyrosine production decrease in the hair bulb.
