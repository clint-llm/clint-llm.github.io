The goal is to get the child to school and to teach parents, school employees, and health care providers to facilitate this.

**Synonyms**

Variously been called school refusal, anxious school refusal, school phobia, or a variant of separation anxiety disorder (SAD).

**Abbreviations**

- ASD: Autism spectrum disorder

- CBT: Cognitive-behavioral therapy

- OCD: Obsessive-compulsive disorder

- ERP: Exposure and response prevention

- DBT: Dialectical behavioral therapy

- SSRI: Selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor

- TCA: Tricyclic antidepressant
