Adverse effects are mainly dose-related and can be described based on the system affected.

- Gastrointestinal: nausea and vomiting (over 50%); abdominal cramps; anorexia - usually, patients are advised to stay hydrated and drink plenty of water while on this therapy.

- Dermatologic: alopecia - it is a universal side effect noted with ifosfamide (90%).

- Central nervous system: encephalopathy (15%).

- Hematologic: leukopenia, anemia, thrombocytopenia (30 to 50%).

- Renal: hematuria - when used alone (90%).

- Endocrine and metabolic: metabolic acidosis (30%).

- Cardiac: arrhythmia (under 10%).

- Hepatic: The severity of liver injury ranges from mild elevations in liver enzymes to massive, fatal hepatic necrosis due to hepatic veno-occlusive disease.
