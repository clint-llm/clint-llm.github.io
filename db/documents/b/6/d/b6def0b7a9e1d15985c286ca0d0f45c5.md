Some differential diagnoses that must be considered in the evaluation of laryngotracheal stenosis include the following:

- Angioedema

- Asthma

- Epiglottitis

- Esophageal tumor

- Foreign body aspiration

- Gastroesophageal reflux disease

- Mediastinal mass

- Retrosternal goiter

- Subglottic web

- Tracheomalacia

- Vocal cord dysfunction
