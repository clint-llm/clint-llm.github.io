An interdisciplinary neonatal team of pediatric neurologists, clinical pediatric neurophysiologists, pediatric critical care specialists, neonatal nurses, and respiratory therapists should work together to achieve an optimal video EEG recording. Documentation of clinical symptoms, current medications, topography, gestational age, corrected age, and state of the infant, among other clinical data, is essential and should be conveyed to the EEG reader for accurate study interpretation.
