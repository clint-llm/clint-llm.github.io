Candesartan is an antihypertensive agent that clinicians also use to manage congestive heart failure. The drug is effective, but prescribers need to be aware of its side effects. The entire interprofessional healthcare team, including clinicians, nurses, and pharmacists, can collaborate to make treatment with candesartan more effective through monitoring therapy, checking dosing, counseling the patient, and watching for drug-drug interactions while minimizing adverse effects, thereby achieving optimal patient outcomes.
