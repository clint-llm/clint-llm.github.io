The prevalence of metachromatic leukodystrophy ranges from 1/40,000 to 1/100,000 in the northern European and North American populations.

- **Late Infantile form**: 6 months to 4 years of age.

- **Juvenile form**:
- Early Juvenile: 4 to 6 years of age.
- Late Juvenile: 6 to 16 years of age.

- **Adult form**: Beyond 16 years of age.
