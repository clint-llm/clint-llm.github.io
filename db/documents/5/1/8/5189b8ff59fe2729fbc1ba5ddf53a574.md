Across all age groups, CHI occurs more often in men than women. Falls cause over half of TBIs in children ages 0 to 14 years old and adults 65 years or older. Among children age 0 to 14, the second leading cause is being struck by or against an object, whereas the second leading cause among other age groups is motor vehicle/traffic injuries. Worldwide, TBI is responsible for greater than 30% of trauma deaths.
