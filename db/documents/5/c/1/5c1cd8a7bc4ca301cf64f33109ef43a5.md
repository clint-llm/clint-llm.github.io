Paraneoplastic cerebellar degeneration has a poor prognosis, Patients with PCD experience progressive symptoms for weeks to months, most patients' symptoms will stabilize after 6 months of onset, by this time the majority of the patients are significantly disabled and are either wheelchair or bed-bound. Patients with anti-yo antibodies and anti-Hu antibodies were noted to have more severe symptoms and are more resistant to treatment and were noted to have worse overall survival compared to patients with anti-Tr antibodies and anti-Ri-antibodies.
