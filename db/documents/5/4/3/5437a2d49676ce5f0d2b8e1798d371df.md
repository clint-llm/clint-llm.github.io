Most of the eyelash conditions discussed here can be diagnosed by taking a careful history and proper clinical examination. Management of the condition will give relief where the eye is threatened.

In the case of trichiasis caused by mucous membrane pemphigoid, it is important to treat the trichiasis but to only undertake surgical correction once appropriate immunomodulation has been commenced. This disease is very unpredictable and demands a careful follow-up by the ophthalmologist, dermatologist, and immunotherapist. Other specialties may need to be involved depending upon the degree of involvement of the other mucosal surface.

Trachoma: Blindness can be prevented by appropriate diagnosis and treatment of this condition.
