Food fortification programs have been implemented in a majority of western countries and have shown to decrease the incidence of folate deficiency. According to the World Health Organization (WHO) along with the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO), the recommended nutrient intake (RNI) for folate is 400mcg per day

Excess intake of folate produces another set of problems, especially in the elderly population. Higher levels of folate have historically been seen to worsen anemia and cognition while masking low levels of vitamin B.
