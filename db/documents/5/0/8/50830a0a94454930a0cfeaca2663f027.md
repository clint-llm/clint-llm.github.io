**Epidural anesthesia -**Injecting anesthetic medications into the epidural space, using a catheter, and multiple dosing is achievable.

**Subarachnoid anesthesia -**anesthetic medications delivered into the subarachnoid space, usually between L3 and L5.

**Lumbar puncture**- Tiny needle through L3 and L5 to obtain CSF from the subarachnoid space for biochemical and microbiology investigations.
