Population awareness regarding the risk of bacterial transmission from mother to fetus is essential to decrease the incidence of this disease. World Meningitis Day on April 24 is intended to raise general awareness.

GBS vaccines are being developed to help prevent neonatal meningitis. Early phase 1 and 2 trial results testing GBS vaccines with maternal vaccination to prevent neonatal meningitis are underway.E coli can potentially decrease neonatal meningitis incidence by two-thirds.
