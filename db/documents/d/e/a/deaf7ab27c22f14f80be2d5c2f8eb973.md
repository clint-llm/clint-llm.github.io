DLCO contraindications:

Measurements of pulmonary diffusing capacity are contraindicated in cases of chest and abdominal pain, oral or facial pain, dementia, or stress incontinence.

The usual recommendation is to postpone pulmonary function testing by a month in cases of acute coronary syndrome or myocardial infarction. PFT is also contraindicated in patients with pneumothorax, ascending aortic aneurysm, pulmonary embolism, severe hypertension, hemoptysis, and major surgeries like thoracic/abdominal /brain/eye/ear/otolaryngological surgery.
