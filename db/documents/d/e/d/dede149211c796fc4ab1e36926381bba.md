Various neurological pathologies can affect preterm and term infants. The signs and symptoms are sometimes non-specific. Knowledge about various clinical signs, a high clinical index of suspicion, and proper clinical examination is necessary to identify these patients who may benefit from imaging such as ultrasound and MRI of the brain.
