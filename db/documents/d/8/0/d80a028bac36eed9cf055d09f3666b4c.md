Prognosis depends on the location of the tumor as well as the stage of the tumor

For intrahepatic bile duct tumors, the 5-year survival is as follows

- Localized tumor or stage I: 15%

- Regional spreading of the tumor seen in Stage II and III: 6%

- Distant metastasis or stage IV: 2%

For Extrahepatic bile duct tumors, the 5-year survival is as follows

- Localized tumor or stage I: 30%

- Regional spreading of the tumor seen in Stage II and III: 24%

- Distant metastasis or stage IV: 2%
