Identifying terminally ill patients with refractory symptoms and timely initiation of goals-of-care discussion are paramount to patient's care. It will not only ensure that patient's wishes are honored but also withhold the use of any redundant therapy or invasive procedure that is unlikely to improve the patient's symptoms or delay disease progression. Prior studies have demonstrated that palliative care has enormous benefits in patients beyond just pain control. These studies reveal the provision of interprofessional care and family meetings enhances the bond between the patient/family, caregiver and relieves stress, anxiety frustration.
