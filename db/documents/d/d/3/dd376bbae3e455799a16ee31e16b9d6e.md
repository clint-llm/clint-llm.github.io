**Goal Outcomes**

- Delivery of a healthy neonate.

- Discharge of mother and baby home.

**Adverse Outcomes**

- Prolonged unconsciousness

- Acute renal failure

- Cerebrovascular accident

- HELLP syndrome

- Pulmonary edema

- Pneumonia

- Coagulopathy

- Abruptio placenta

- Cortical blindness

- Cardiomegaly

- Vesicovaginal fistula

- Death
