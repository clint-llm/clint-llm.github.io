Hormone replacement therapy is a mainstay of treatment for those suffering from opioid-induced endocrinopathy (OIE) which can have a significant impact on a patient's health and quality of life. This activity reviews the role of the inter-professional team in the evaluation and management of OIE.

**Objectives:**
- Describe the pathophysiology of opioid-induced endocrinopathy.
- Outline the typical presentation of a patient with opioid-induced endocrinopathy.
- Summarize the treatment of a patient with opioid-induced endocrinopathy.
- Identify the role of the interprofessional team in the evaluation and management of the opioid-induced endocrinopathy.
