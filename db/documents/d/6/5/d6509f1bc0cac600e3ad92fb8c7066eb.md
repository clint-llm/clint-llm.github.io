One of the contraindications of cephalosporin is if patients are allergic to them or have had an anaphylactic reaction to penicillin or other beta-lactam antimicrobials.

Ceftriaxone is contraindicated in neonates with hyperbilirubinemia because of reports that ceftriaxone displaces bilirubin from albumin, increasing the free bilirubin concentrations and increasing the risk of jaundice in neonates.
