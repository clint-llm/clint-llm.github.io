Management of HIV patients is best accomplished with the help of an interprofessional team of healthcare professionals that includes the infectious disease specialist, pharmacist, internist, infection control specialty nurses, and other specialists depending on the organ infected. This approach demonstrates the best chances for optimal patient care and therapeutic success. [Level V]

The pharmacist has to emphasize medication compliance and regular follow up of HIV seropositive individuals. Safe sex practices, needle disposal education in intravenous drug users are highly encouraged to reduce the spread of HIV infection.
