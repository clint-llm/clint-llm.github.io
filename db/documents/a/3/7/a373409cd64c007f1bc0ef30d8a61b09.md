The presentation of duodenal trauma is nonspecific, with many other etiologies presenting similarly.

- Esophageal trauma

- Stomach trauma

- Small bowel (jejunum or ileum) trauma

- Colorectal trauma

- Mesenteric trauma

- Mesenteric hematoma

- Intraabdominal vascular trauma

- Pancreas trauma

- Liver trauma

- Spleen trauma

- Kidney trauma

- Bladder trauma
