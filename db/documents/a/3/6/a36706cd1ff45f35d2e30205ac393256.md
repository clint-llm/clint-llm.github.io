In addition to PTH resistance, PHP1A and PHP1C are also associated with resistance to other hormones such as thyroid-stimulating hormone (TSH), growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH), gonadotropins, and calcitonin.

Short stature, a common feature of AHO, results due to various reasons. Few patients may have intrauterine growth retardation, but the growth deficit becomes more apparent over time due to reduced growth velocity after few years of birth, compromised pubertal growth spurt, and premature cessation of growth due to early fusion of epiphyses.
