Cryptogenic cirrhosis can be a life-altering disease. For those patients at risk or who have a family history of liver disease, obesity, diabetes, hypertension, or hyperlipidemia are encouraged to see their primary care physician routinely. If one is at high-risk of cryptogenic cirrhosis, medication compliance, lifestyle and diet modifications, and surveillance are important.
