The exact etiology of osteochondritis dissecans of the knee has not been confirmed, and there are no clear guidelines for the prevention or deterrence of osteochondritis dissecans of the knee. Patients with osteochondritis dissecans of the knee who are between the ages of 10 and 20 should receive education and encouragement regarding the high success rates of conservative management. Adults with osteochondritis dissecans of the knee should be informed that there is a high likelihood surgical therapy will be required to help them fully recover.
