Excisional biopsy or punch biopsy with Gram stain, culture, and histopathologic examination can be performed in equivocal cases. In cases of pseudomonal EG, Gram stain shows many gram-negative, rod-shaped bacteria.P. aeruginosa but may reveal a different causative organism. Microscopic examination reveals an ulcerated zone of necrotic epidermis and dermis with neutrophilic predominance.
