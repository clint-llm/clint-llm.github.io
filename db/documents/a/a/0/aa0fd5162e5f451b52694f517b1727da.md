Hydroa vacciniforme is a rare photosensitivity disorder of childhood. Hydroa vacciniforme-like lymphoma is a more aggressive and fatal variant of hydroa vacciniforme. This activity reviews the evaluation and management of hydroa vacciniforme and highlights the role of the interprofessional team in improving care for patients with this condition.

**Objectives:**
- Identify the etiology of hydroa vacciniforme medical conditions and emergencies.
- Describe the history and physical findings expected in hydroa vacciniforme.
- Outline the management options available for hydroa vacciniforme.
- Review interprofessional team strategies for improving care coordination and communication to address hydroa vacciniforme and improve outcomes.
