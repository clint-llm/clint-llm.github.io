Patients suffering from medullary thyroid cancer has clinical presentation similar to other thyroid diseases, hence the following differential must be kept in mind while evaluating these patients.

- Anaplastic thyroid carcinoma

- De Quervain thyroiditis

- Follicular thyroid carcinoma

- Graves disease

- Intestinal carcinoid tumor

- Multiple endocrine neoplasia (MEN) Type 2

- Papillary thyroid carcinoma

- Thyroid lymphoma

- Thyroid nodule

- Thyrotoxicosis

- Toxic nodular goiter
