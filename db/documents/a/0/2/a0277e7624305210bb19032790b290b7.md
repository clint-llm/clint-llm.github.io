The use of diagnostic ultrasound and Doppler imaging has become ubiquitous in the clinical environment. Doppler ultrasound is a fast, safe, and useful modality used to obtain crucial information for a wide range of pathologies. It is essential for all individuals who have a role in clinical care to understand the utility of ultrasound in diagnosis and therapeutics. It is helpful for health care team members to have a shared understanding of indications for utilizing Doppler ultrasound as well as downstream impacts on clinical care and medical decision-making.
