In general, differential diagnosis of pheochromocytomas is broad, and may include but not be limited to adrenal pheochromocytomas, hyperthyroidism, thyroid storm, cardiac arrhythmias, carcinoid tumor, migraine, stroke, medullary thyroid carcinoma, mastocytosis, etc.

In patients with a confirmed cardiac mass, the differential diagnosis may include pericardial mass, abscess, or metastasis.
