Those patients who have idiopathic intracranial hypertension and are overweight should be advised to lose weight because the loss of as little as 6% of body weight can terminate the disorder. Patients should also be educated regarding the potential for disabling blindness therefore they should consult an ophthalmologist soon for any visual disturbance.
