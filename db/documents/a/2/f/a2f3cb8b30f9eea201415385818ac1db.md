The exact etiology of Takotsubo cardiomyopathy is not fully understood. Several mechanisms are hypothesized as possible etiologies of Takotsubo cardiomyopathy and include sympathetic overdrive with increased catecholamines, coronary spasm, microvascular dysfunction, low estrogen levels, inflammation, or impaired myocardial fatty acid metabolism.
